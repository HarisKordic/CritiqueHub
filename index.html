What is CritiqueHub?

CritiqueHub combines the best elements of platforms like Yelp, Facebook, and Reddit, tailored to meet the needs of businesses and users across Europe. It offers features such as:

  • Interactive Reactions from Facebook
  • Threaded Replies from Reddit
  • Comprehensive Business Profiles to showcase products and services
  • Reviews with Ratings, Comments, Images, and Replies
  • A Messaging System to connect users and businesses seamlessly
